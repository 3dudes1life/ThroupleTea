# ThroupleTea.com SEO Audit — July 14, 2026

Exact searches performed for:
- site:throupletea.com
- site:throupletea.com "A Little Throuple Tea"
- "A Little Throuple Tea" "throupletea.com"

did not surface throupletea.com in the returned search results during this audit.

This does not prove that no URL is indexed in every Google data center, but it is a strong sign that the domain is not currently earning meaningful organic visibility.

## Critical issues found in the GitHub repo
1. No robots.txt.
2. No sitemap.xml.
3. Homepage had no canonical URL.
4. Homepage lacked PodcastSeries, FAQPage, WebSite and Organization structured data.
5. The placeholder GTM-XXXXXXX script attempted to load a nonexistent Tag Manager container.
6. Important keyword context existed, but it was scattered and not organized into a clear homepage information hierarchy.
7. The existing SEO pages were not reinforced by a strong crawlable footer and homepage content cluster.
8. The homepage title prioritized the temporary phrase "Season 2 Streaming Now" over durable search intent.
9. The homepage hero artwork lacked explicit width and height, increasing layout-shift risk.
10. Internal discovery paths existed but were not grouped into a strong topical hub.
