About this episode

This week we're talking about all the random things we were convinced meant someone was rich growing up... and looking back, we couldn't have been more wrong.

From two-story houses and homemade lemonade to Gateway computers, deli meat from the grocery store, and Sleep Number beds, we're reminiscing about the childhood "luxuries" that made us think someone had officially made it.

Naturally, the conversation goes completely off the rails when Daniel accidentally says "Poke Your Anus" instead of Pocahontas, Will pretends to know what Cap Rock is, and somehow decides they're the hottest accessory of the season.

We also catch up on Daniel becoming a Reiki Master, the launch of our new website, this weekend's Out at the Fair® at the Orange County Fair, and our signed book giveaway.

Plus, we answer new Throuple Hotline questions from listeners navigating relationship drama, family obligations, and protecting your own peace.

📞 Is it selfish to skip family events when your social battery is completely drained?
