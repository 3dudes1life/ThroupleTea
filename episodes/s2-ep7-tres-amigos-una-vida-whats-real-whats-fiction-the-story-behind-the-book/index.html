About this episode

Is  Tres Amigos, Una Vida  a true story… or fiction?

In Episode 7 of  A Little Throuple Tea , we finally unpack the question we get asked constantly:

This episode goes deeper than the surface. We talk about why we wrote the book in the first place, what parts of our real lives inspired it, what was changed for storytelling, and why the ending of Book One was never meant to be the end.

Because  Tres Amigos, Una Vida  was never designed as a single story — it’s the beginning of a much bigger universe.
