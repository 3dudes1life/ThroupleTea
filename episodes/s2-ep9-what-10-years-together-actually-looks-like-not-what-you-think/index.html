About this episode

What does 10 years of love actually look like?

Not the highlight reel. Not the fantasy. The real version.

In Episode 9 of  A Little Throuple Tea , we talk about what long-term love actually becomes after the early excitement fades — and why that’s not a bad thing.

We break down the difference between what people  think  a throuple relationship looks like versus what it actually takes to maintain one for a decade.

From the hard years filled with growth, ego clashes, and outside pressure… to the quieter, “boring” seasons built on routine, stability, and showing up for each other — this episode is honest, grounded, and real.

Because long-term love isn’t built on constant excitement.
