Be a Guest on A Little Throuple Tea

We’re always looking for incredible voices to join us on the couch. If you’ve got a story, lived experience, relationship insight, chaos, wisdom, or you’re just funny as hell — we want to hear from you.

Filming takes place on select Sundays, scheduled by mutual availability ✨

Who We’re Looking For

We’re inviting guests from every corner of the LGBTQ+ universe — whether you're part of a throuple, navigating nontraditional relationships, building something bold, or just living loudly.

  • ✨ LGBTQ+ singles, couples, throuples & chosen family
  • ✨ People living nontraditional relationship styles
  • ✨ Creators, small biz owners, queer artists & activists
  • ✨ Anyone with wild, heartfelt, inspiring, funny, or messy stories
  • ✨ Everyday people with something to say

We film in San Diego — but you do NOT need to be local.
Guests can join in person or remotely via video call 💻✨

What to Expect

  • 📅 Filming on select Sundays, scheduled by mutual agreement
  • 🎥 Filming takes about 1 hour
  • 🎙 Episodes are usually 40–70 minutes
  • 📍 In-person in San Diego or remote via video
  • ☕️ Casual, funny, unfiltered — nothing formal
  • 💅 No media experience needed — just vibes
