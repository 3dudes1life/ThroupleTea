About the show

A Little Throuple Tea is a gay throuple podcast hosted by real-life partners William, Caleb & Daniel — three men in one committed relationship sharing the unfiltered truth about love, chaos, and everything in between.

From navigating polyamory and modern LGBTQ relationships to money talk, haunted houses, pop culture meltdowns, family dynamics, and very real throuple arguments — nothing is off limits.

Equal parts heartfelt and unhinged, this isn’t a relationship advice podcast pretending to be perfect. It’s real conversations, messy moments, sharp humor, and honest insight into what it actually looks like to build a life as a three-person partnership.

New episodes drop every Friday. Whether you're curious about nontraditional relationships, already living in one, or just here for the chaos — welcome to the tea.
