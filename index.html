About the show

3Dudes1Life: A Little Throuple Tea is where love, chaos, and laughter collide. Join real-life throuple William, Caleb, and Daniel as they spill stories from their unconventional life — the wins, the wild nights, and the “did that really just happen?” moments. Nothing’s off limits: relationships, friendships, family drama, pop culture, and the mess in between. Equal parts heart and humor, this is your weekly sip of throuple life that proves love doesn’t fit in a box.. Segments like Smut TV, listener Qs, and “real‑life vs the book” keep it spicy.

  • New episodes drop mid‑September
  • Weekly, ~25–45 minutes
  • Recorded in San Diego
