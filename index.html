What is a throuple—and what does one actually look like in real life?

A throuple is a romantic relationship involving three people. Some throuples are open, some are polyamorous in broader ways and some—like William, Caleb and Daniel—are committed exclusively to one another as a closed triad.

The podcast is not a clinical relationship manual. It is a first-person record of what happens when three men build a home, make decisions, manage conflict and choose each other over and over again.
