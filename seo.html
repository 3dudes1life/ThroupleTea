A Little Throuple Tea

A Little Throuple Tea is a gay throuple podcast hosted by William, Caleb, and Daniel — a real-life LGBTQ throuple sharing honest conversations about love, relationships, polyamory, nontraditional partnerships, queer culture, and everyday chaos.

About the Podcast

This LGBTQ podcast explores what it means to live in a committed three-person relationship. From navigating jealousy and communication to money talk, pop culture, and chaotic group chat stories — nothing is off limits.

Listeners searching for a gay relationship podcast, polyamory podcast, throuple advice, or LGBTQ storytelling will find authentic, funny, and unfiltered conversations here.
